The original Tsui Wah restaurant located in Central District, Hong Kong

When I think of Hong Kong food, Tsui Wah restaurant 翠華餐廳 comes to mind. It is a predecessor of tea cafes from 1967, and it began as a "bing sut"冰室. It is known as a "cha chaan teng", 茶餐廳, which provides local Hong Kong cuisine. It is a staple of Hong Kong local culture, and is a convenient and fast restaurant. Its branch at Central Wellington Road has become a default meeting/gathering place for many people, especially if they need late night suppers after a night of partying in Lan Kwai Fong.
