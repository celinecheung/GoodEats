SEOUL, KOREA
Saebyukjib
새벽집

The 24/7 Korean BBQ restaurant's exterior.

Saebyukjib means House of Dawn in Korean. This meaning holds some truth, as this restaurant is a favorite for after-hour classic Korean food. The restaurant is open 24/7. It is known for having one of the best quality meat in Seoul, offering them in Korean barbeque and other classic Korean dishes.

Saebyukjib is known for its high quality meat, especially for its yook-hwe, sashimi-style fresh raw beef ((₩28,000 +10% service charge), and its yook-hwe bimbimbap (₩8,000), a bowl of rice in a hot pot topped with raw beef, vegetables. It is often served with hot sauce. Another must-try dish is bulgogi marinated beef BBQ, which is thinly sliced beef marinated in pear juice, garlic, soy sauce and other seasonings (depending on your favor).
